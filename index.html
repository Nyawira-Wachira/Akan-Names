About

This application takes in your birthday details and your gender, calculates the particular day you were born, then assigns you an Akan name.

Akan names come from the Ghanian culture where children are given names depending on the day of the week they are born, and of course the gender to which they belong.

FIND OUT WHAT YOUR AKAN NAME IS.

Fill in your birthday details and choose your gender then click submit to get your Akan name.
